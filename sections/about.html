About Mintcha

Mintcha is a modern beverage brand established in Malaysia with the vision of reinventing how people enjoy refreshing drinks. We specialize in mint-inspired beverages, blending premium tea, coffee, fruit, and plant-based ingredients to create drinks that are both unique and familiar.

Vision

To become Malaysia’s leading lifestyle beverage brand by introducing refreshing, innovative drinks for modern consumers.

Mission

  • Deliver refreshing mint-infused beverages that combine global and local flavors.
  • Provide commuters with convenient, quick, and high-quality drinks.
  • Champion sustainability by offering eco-friendly packaging and plant-based alternatives.

Inspired by the simple joys in life – two friends sharing Yong Tau Foo at MRT Ampang Park – Mintcha started with a mission to make drinks that brighten your day.
